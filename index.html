Instructions

This game is as simple as it gets. Boxes with different colors will appear on your screen, you have to beat the boxes with the color same as that of the difficulty button you have chosen.There are two modes of the game, mouse mode and keyboard mode. Choose the mode you are comfortable with. You would get three lives, indicated by the hearts on the top. Once you run out of lives, the game ends and your time is your score. Click on the logo above to return to home screen. Now choose your difficulty level and beat the shit out of those boxes!




What Do You Prefer?

In mouse mode click on the boxes of the right color and in the keyboard mode press the respective letter appearing on the box of the right color. If on a mobile device, choose mouse mode.
